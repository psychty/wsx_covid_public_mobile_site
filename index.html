Hospital activity

This section includes what we know about the number of people being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 or catching the virus whilst in hospital as well as the number of COVID-19 positive patients currently occupying beds

Note, this data relates to the hospital trusts providiing care and does not necessarily reflect the residents of a particular area.

Deaths

The Office for National Statistics published weekly data on the number of deaths occurring each week in 2020 and whether or not COVID-19 was mentioned as an underlying or contributing factor to the death.

Note, this is not the same as mortality reporting on the government website which includes any deaths within 28 days of a positive test result.
